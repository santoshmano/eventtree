Location

Have you decided to celebrate your event at home, work or at some other Location?

We help you organize your event at home or work. But if you are looking to have it at another location we are here to help you find and choose beautiful locations. We work with the space owners to book and provide you a great price and service

Activity

We help you find and rent fun activities for your event

We help you find fun activities to do during your event which may be at your home/work or outside. Looking for Bouncer/Jumper Houses, Interactive Games etc? or other outdoor activities as part of your event like animal petting, educational, pony rides, train rides etc?

Theme

Want your party/event to have a certain Theme?

Themed parties are great, we help you create a certain theme for your party. We can help find decorators to setup your space, and Artists who will make your theme come to life!

Entertainers

Looking for Entertainers and Artists to add color to you party?

We work with great Entertainers like Magicians, Face Painters, Balloon Artists, Musicians and others. We would love for you to host and add fun to your party.

Food

Do you want help to find the right food for your event?

We can help you find suitable restaraunts/caterers for you to order your party food at. Or we can help you find the right caterers who can not only provide great food, but also add some life to your party.

Packages

Looking to find and book specific services, or complete packages?

We help you find and book specific services to fill up your event, or help you find complete packages. Either ways the goal be you are on budget, and have a successful event.
